home

a breaking wave

about to leave my place, but hoping to rip out a quick post before doing so. i've been reflecting some lately on a feeling that i've been having during some conversations.

sometimes this feel rises up inside me and then subsides like a swelling wave in the ocean that never breaks. and sometimes this feeling raises it's head and demands to be known. regardless, this feeling of mine is not happy with me. it is a feeling like there is not enough air in the conversation. and that i gasp for it as soon as it becomes available. it is a feeling of veiled desparation, of trying hold on to more than i can handle.

i think i know a big part of the reason this feeling is not happy with me but if i speak on it i will probably say a whole lot of nothing.

i'll say this... this feeling of mine is telling me to be someone a little different than what i've had in mind. i suppose i will listen.
